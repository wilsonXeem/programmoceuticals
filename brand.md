# ProgrammoCeuticals — Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Overview

Brand Name: ProgrammoCeuticals

ProgrammoCeuticals is a technology education and solutions company focused on equipping professionals—especially pharmacists and pharmacy students—with structured, practical, and industry-relevant technical skills.

The brand operates at the intersection of:

- Healthcare
- Technology
- Education
- Regulation

The brand tone is professional, academic, and purpose-driven.

---

## 2. Logo Usage

Primary Logo:

- The brand name "ProgrammoCeuticals" used as a wordmark
- No symbol, icon, or abstract mark is to be created or used

Rules:

- Use the name alone as the logo
- Do not create or introduce a logo icon
- Do not stylize the name excessively
- Maintain adequate whitespace around the wordmark
- The logo should appear in the header, aligned left

Approved Variations:

- Blue text on white background
- White text on blue background

---

## 3. Brand Colors

### Primary Colors

Blue:

- HEX: #0B5ED7
- RGB: 11, 94, 215
- Usage:
  - Headings
  - Navigation elements
  - Buttons and call-to-action elements
  - Accents and highlights

White:

- HEX: #FFFFFF
- Usage:
  - Primary background color
  - Content sections
  - Spacing and layout clarity

### Neutral Support Color

Dark Gray:

- HEX: #2E2E2E
- Usage:
  - Body text
  - Secondary text elements

Restrictions:

- Do not introduce additional brand colors
- Do not use gradients, neon colors, or decorative palettes

---

## 4. Typography

Primary Font:

- Inter (Google Font)

Font Usage:

- Headings: Inter SemiBold or Bold
- Body text: Inter Regular
- Buttons and UI text: Inter Medium

Fallback Fonts:

- System UI
- Helvetica
- Arial

Restrictions:

- Do not use decorative, script, or handwritten fonts
- Do not mix more than two fonts
- Prioritize readability and clarity

---

## 5. Visual & UI Style

Design Principles:

- Clean and minimal layout
- White background dominance
- Blue used sparingly and intentionally
- Clear hierarchy and generous spacing
- Institutional and academic feel

UI Constraints:

- No flashy animations
- No excessive rounded corners
- No gradients or visual clutter
- Design should feel calm, serious, and credible

Target Aesthetic:

- Professional education institution
- Regulated-industry friendly
- Long-term and timeless (not trendy)

---

## 6. Tone & Voice

Tone:

- Professional
- Calm
- Confident
- Educational

Voice Characteristics:

- Clear and structured
- Direct and respectful
- Focused on competence and value
- Non-hype, non-salesy

Avoid:

- Slang or casual language
- Buzzwords without substance
- Marketing exaggeration
- Emojis in formal contexts

---

## 7. Content Guidelines

Content Style:

- Informative and instructional
- Structured with headings and sections
- Focus on learning outcomes and impact
- Emphasize depth over speed

Audience:

- Pharmacy students
- Pharmacists
- Professionals transitioning into tech
- Institutions and academic bodies

---

## 8. Brand Positioning (Internal Reference)

ProgrammoCeuticals positions itself as a serious, structured, and credible institution for technical education, particularly within healthcare and regulated professional environments.

The brand prioritizes:

- Long-term competence
- Ethical practice
- Practical skills
- Academic rigor

---

## 9. Implementation Note (For Developers & AI Tools)

All designs, interfaces, documents, and websites must:

- Adhere strictly to these guidelines
- Avoid introducing unapproved styles or elements
- Treat this document as authoritative

When in doubt, choose clarity, simplicity, and professionalism.
